Western Vietnam Experience – Journey to the Land of Rivers

Posted on July 15, 2025 • by Ánh Phụng

The Western region of Vietnam is not only about Can Tho with its Ninh Kieu Wharf or the bustling Cai Rang floating market. It is also a journey through fruit-laden orchards in Tien Giang, the rustic village paths of Ben Tre, or the echoing melodies of traditional "vong co" music across the green fields of An Giang. Further south, Ca Mau emerges as the southernmost tip of the country – where the land meets the sea, vast mangrove forests thrive, and kind-hearted, simple people live.

This article is a space for you to share your impressions, stories, photos, and honest reviews of your journey through the land of rivers – from Can Tho to Ca Mau and across the entire Mekong Delta.

🌿 A Friendly Note When Visiting the Mekong Delta

Wake up early to catch the floating market at sunrise. Don’t hesitate to ride a small boat or cycle along the peaceful village paths. And remember, if you're offered fresh fruits from the orchard – don’t refuse, as it’s the Mekong people's way of showing heartfelt hospitality.
