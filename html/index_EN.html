Southwest Vietnam

The Waterway Region of the Southwest

The Mekong Delta, also known as the Southwest region of Vietnam, is a fertile land with a vast network of rivers, endless rice fields, and lush fruit orchards. Here, you will see bustling boats on rivers, vibrant floating markets from early morning, and kind, hospitable people. Southwestern cuisine is rustic yet flavorful—from fragrant grilled snakehead fish, crispy pancakes (bánh xèo), to sweet tropical fruits like durian, mango, and rambutan. Traveling through the Southwest is a slow-paced experience: rowing a sampan through melaleuca forests, listening to folk songs on the river, or simply relaxing to the peaceful rhythm of rural life. The region may not be flashy, but it's genuine and simple. Once you visit, you'll surely remember the golden sunshine, the aroma of ripened rice, and the radiant smiles of the locals.
